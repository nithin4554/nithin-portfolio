Brand Concept Pitch

Creative Strategy for Malaaha

Malaaha is rooted in self-love, healing, and bodycare ritual. Here is a conceptual blueprint showing how we can elevate Malaaha's digital footprint using emotional storytelling and high-engagement short-form campaigns.

UGC & Community Campaign Insight: Malaaha is rooted in self-love, so audience participation should feel personal, reflective, and easy to join.

30 Days of Body Confidence

A 30-day UGC challenge where users share one small self-love or bodycare ritual each day. It moves the conversation from product features to personal habits, generating authentic trust and massive community repost material.

  • Execution: Brand-themed IG stories templates, daily micro-prompts, and weekly community spotlights.
  • Why it fits: Builds brand affinity and makes self-love feel interactive and collective.

Confidence Calendar

Click days to preview prompt
Day 1

Apply lotion with intentional strokes, focusing on feeling gratitude for your body. Share a skin-texture snap using #MalaahaConfidences.

Short-form Video Series Insight: Bodycare content performs better when it becomes part of an emotional routine, not just a functional demo.

Night-time Ritual Stories

A short-form series showing calming night routines using Malaaha products. Instead of energetic tutorials, this uses ASMR, soft warm lighting, and reflective affirmations that soothe the viewer after a long day.

  • Execution: YouTube Shorts and IG Reels with warm closeups, soothing audio textures, and minimalist text overlays.
  • Why it fits: Rebrands Malaaha from "skincare bottles" to "indispensable night-time therapy".
"I am healing. My skin is my canvas, and I treat it with love."
Testimonial Campaign Insight: Malaaha speaks to real concerns like body acne, uneven skin tone, and pigmentation. Honest storytelling builds brand authority.

Your Skin, Your Story

A campaign built on vulnerability and skin-positive diaries. Real customers and brand friends talk about their skin struggles and how rituals helped them feel comfortable in their own skin.

  • Execution: Founder-led dialogues, customer journal carousels, and unretouched photo layouts.
  • Why it fits: Differentiates Malaaha as a brand of trust, transparency, and body positivity.
